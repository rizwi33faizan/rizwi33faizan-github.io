The Cultural Fest

The biggest and the most awaited cultural week of NIT Silchar, Incandescence, is a platform of sheer exultation. Boasting of active participation from the people of all-over the north east, Incandescence, is famous for spotting, finding and developing hidden talents. In Incandescence, we organise events such as DEPRADOR, where skilled debaters from all over India compete to be crowned.

The Technical Fest

Tecnoesis is one of the largest and the highly anticipated techno-management fest of North-eastern India.In Tecnoesis,we organise interactive lecture sessions led by distinguished guests from all over the country along with elite technical workshops based on the latest trends. There are also many technical exhibitions to give the visitors a deep insight on new trending technologies.

The POSUA

We have POSUA, a cultural weekend based on the rich and ever warming cultural heritage of Assam. Organised during the season of Bohag Bihu, it marks the beginning of the Hindu Solar Calender. It is a day of frolic, entertainment and feasting. Popular singers like Dikshu, are invited for the heartfelt celebrations of Bihu in the campus of NIT Silchar.
